Colourful India

Julian Wan | 2020/07/06

Julian Wan is a Creative Director and Co-Founder at IdentityPop.com, dog dad. Big nerd and usually doing web dev stuff.

I love visiting different countries to witness various kinds of cultures and traditions. India is a country that is based known for its unique diversity of people and culture. With the large differences in culture, comes a ton of different cultural practices which make India a truly coulourful and vibrant country. In India the religious festivals of all religions are celebrated with equal vigour, be it Holi, Durga Puja, Ganesh Chaturthi, Christmas or id. The celebration of the different cultures together as one is what makes India a vibrant and colourful country.

India is my favourite country to visit for all seasons. Whatever the season, India is always beautiful and welcoming!

Hope to bring the beauty of India and it's people to the whole world through my blogs. It is a captivating beast that you won't be able to take your eyes off again. Explore the country in your own pace and discover things you have never seen before.
